Our Vision

Sonera Magnetics is democratizing portable, high-performance functional brain imaging. Sonera’s technology could transform room-sized diagnostic machines into a wearable device, at just a fraction of the cost, and ultimately enable the first practical, non-invasive portable brain-computer interface for consumer, military and industrial use.

Our Technology & Applications

Our core technology, based on acoustically-driven ferromagnetic resonance, is a magnetic sensor which leverages the strong interaction between GHz-frequency sound waves and magnetic thin films. This coupling enables a device with performance comparable to that of SQUID and SERF magnetometers, but that is capable of operating in ambient environmental conditions, ultimately leading to a dramatic reduction in size, weight, power consumption and cost, as compared to existing commercial solutions.

Medical Diagnostics
‍Sonera’s portable brain imaging systems can be used to provide concordant evidence for the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders, such as epilepsy and dementia.
‍
Brain-computer Interfaces
‍Many of today’s BCI technologies rely on noisy, distorted electrical signals or invasive implantation of electrodes into the brain. Sonera’s technology can detect high-quality magnetic signals from neurons firing in the brain using a non-invasive, low-cost and low-power system - enabling a truly practical brain-computer interface.

Our story

Founded in 2018, Sonera Magnetics is a team of technical experts and visionaries driven by the mission of expanding access to the wealth of information in the brain through portable, high-resolution brain imaging systems. We believe that novel technological solutions like ours can unlock significant advancements in the treatment of neurological disorders and our fundamental understanding of the brain, as well as ultimately change the way we interface with the world. Sonera Magnetics is based in Berkeley, CA.
