Hi, my name is Cal – I'm a Product Designer.

Recently, I have been working with multiple tech start-ups in the Bay Area, where I provide end-to-end product design solutions to optimize their products across a variety of digital platforms.‍

I live and breath design. Ever since I was a kid, I was curious about how things were designed. So, given my massive childhood collection of "How it's Made" books, it wasn't a surprise I would attend USC's School of Architecture. At USC, I continued exploring my curiosity of understanding how humans interacted with their environment.

Now, I continue this passion by building interactive digital experiences. On the side, I enjoy photography, painting murals, building temples and doing photorealistic drawings. Last but not least, I love having Ramen with friends until the AM's.
