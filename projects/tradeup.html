Project Overview

Sonera didn't have designs for their brand touchpoints

I was commissioned by Sonera Magnetics to develop a brand identity from the ground up. The company is researching magnetic sensors that will be used in several applications such as medical, military, and transportation.  Some design elements drew inspiration from waveforms upon which the company’s core technology is based.  All materials were used to engage with investors at neurotechnology events in the Bay Area.
