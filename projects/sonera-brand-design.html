SONERA MAGENETICS

Brand Design

Sonera is developing technology for brain-computer interfaces to untether the hidden information from the human brain.

The two co-founders reached out to me to develop a brand from the ground up. Together, we developed the overall feel of the brand. Afterward, I designed the brand identity with all touchpoints. The materials are used to engage with investors at high-profile neurotechnology events in the Bay Area.
